Anoma Sutta
Discourse on the Possessor of Numerous Worthy Epithets

      45. (The deva said)

      "Behold and worship the All-knowing, the possessor of numerous worthy epithets, the perceiver of the subtle sense, the giver of Insight, the one who is not attached to sense-pleasures, the Great Sage, the Seeker-After-Perfection (in Morality, Concentration and Wisdom), who walks the Ariya Path"

End of the Anoma Sutta,

the fifth in this vagga
